Lemaire란 무엇인가?

Helmed by Christophe Lemaire and Sarah-Linh Tran, Lemaire house brings together a team united by their shared belief in a wardrobe approach focused on versatile pieces designed for everyday life.

Subtle, understated cuts and superior materials place these pieces squarely within a tradition of a cosmopolitan and sophisticated Parisian style.

Each collection is designed and developed in the historic Marais neighbourhood of Paris, not far from the Lemaire flagship boutique on 28 rue de Poitou.
